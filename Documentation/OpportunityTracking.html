Opportunity Tracking


The Opportunity Tracking tool is used to monitor and view opportunity tracking list which are added by the administrators, the list is similar to the sales opportunity tracking used as a management tool in most CRM systems to monitor business opportunity which are categorized in terms of Stages, Type, Sources and others depending on the needs of the business.


Because of the importance of opportunity tracking to monitor and track activates to the particular opportunity/client an activity log is also implemented to easily track meetings and other value adding activity related to that opportunity.


The rightmost portion of the table shows two icons, a Edit/Update and Scheduler Icon denoted by an calender.



The Edit/Update button redirects to this page which enables administrators to update opportunity details if necessary, say administrators are advised to update opportunity rating and change in stage for ease of monitoring



Clicking then in the Scheduler/Calendar icon redirects the administrator to a scheduler tool which lets administrators add activity schedules particular to the opportunity in the previous list. To add a activity/event on the scheduler, the administrator right clicks on the time line date




The administrator then fills up the specific fields particular to the activity/event such as location, start/end time, label, and description.



One of the other available views available to the scheduler are Day, Work Week, Full Week, Month and Time line. Whichever is appropriate for the opportunity being track is available for the administrators to use.



Adding a new opportunity for tracking is similar as to updating one, filling up the required fields and submitting for further tracking in the future.
