Administrator Module

Accessing the administrator module requires the user prompt of administrator credentials.



The Administrator dashboard has a similar resemblance to the Account dashboard used by customer, similarity in the layout and and color scheme but indeed has more features and functionalities for the administrators of the system.



The dashboard itself has a summary count of many features  such as

  • Total users and count of new users in the last 24 hours
  • Total unread messages and new in the last 24 hours
  • Total survey submissions and those new in the last 24 hours
  • New renewal submissions and those new in the last 24 hours
  • Deposit/Payment slip uploads and those new in the last 24 hours
  • Total log counts and again, new in the last 24 hours



Another summary which can also be seen in the dashboard is the sum of payments via deposit/payment slip uploads in the last 24 hours, the summary includes a span date. Which are computed as 24 hours minus the current time.


The second box of summary is a 30 days count of total deposit/payment slip uploads.


The next row then shows a sum total of corporate payments computed in the last 30 days on the time of viewing on the dashboard by the administrator, as seen in the second paragraph showing the span of date.


The next box shows a summary of balance of payables by corporate memberships.



A visual representation using charts to provide a summary of renewals sent in last 7 days, including the count of sent, paid and unpaid renewals.


Below the pie charts gives a representation of fractions of Membership compositions and User composition for the administrators to view.



A strip of summary of recent messages can be seen by the administrators seating beside in the left of the previous summaries. This shows the category of message, time and date of submission, senders name and a summary of the message.



Like the Account Module, the administrator can also update their profile information similar to the customers accounts.
