Automatic Membership Renewal


The Automatic Membership Renewal page can reflect somewhat similar to the renewal form, but has some fixed options such as the renewal length which are fixed to 1 year and the type of membership which is limited to Individual in the time of writing. For customer without previous memberships, this feature is disabled.


A new option in this form includes the Notify Me which lets the customer to pick a prior date which a reminder email will be automatically sent to the customer .

Yet another email will also be sent if the customer choses to proceed with the membership by leaving it to enabled.
