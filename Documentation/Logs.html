Logs list


The logs list compromises of sub modules namely


  • Feedback logs
  • Renewal Submissions logs
  • Renewal Voids logs
  • Deposit Slip Submissions logs
  • Exception logs


The different submodules sorts the logs into each category as to how its titled for better organization. The feedback, renewal and exception logs also has reports functionality similar to the other master list discussed earlier.



The renewal logs lets the administrator directly access the particular renewal details by clicking the rightmost part of the table.
