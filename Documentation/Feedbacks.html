Feedbacks


The Feedbacks page compromises of three sub pages namely the Feedback, Survey and Issue/Inquiry pages. This has its own uses as the name suggest.



This page can be used by the customers to submit feedbacks specific to some included in the Feedback category drop down list as seen the capture above

or other topics. Furthermore, customers can write detailed feedbacks or comments regarding the feedback category if it seems necessary for them.



Upon successful submission of the feedback, the system will automatically send a email prompt confirmation the submission of the feedback.




The survey page enables users to answer to answer a quick ten question survey regarding a specific feature or service as indicated by the company,

it also includes a comment box for customers to enter further description of their comments/suggestions.



In the Issue & Inquiries page, customers then can enter inquiries regarding the companies services and/or corrections regarding information and details as

seen anywhere in the page. As the system is new, bugs and error can be encountered once in a while so customers are encouraged to submit and errors

problems they encounter for the company to improve and attend to.



Similarly, the successful submission on the Issue & Inquiry page will prompt an automatic email from the system regarding the submission, which then the a customer care representative from the company will contact the recipient using the email used for submission.
