Membership Renewal


The Membership Renewal page, or Renewal Form is used to renewal previous client memberships. The page requires first, users to verify their information as this will by registered as their billing address and used in responding to their emergency for home dispatch. Again a valid email address is required as to the system will send a membership receipt regarding the membership renewal. A prompt for password is also required for the user to enter for security purposes. On the upper right portion, are details regarding the renewal which are required by the customer to enter.


The two types of memberships which the customer can choose from are the Individual and Household membership


Individual memberships cost P 1000.00 /year of memberships in the time of writing, Household memberships which includes membership for four household members cost P 3000.00 /year.


(price are subject to change without prior notice, refer to the website for price changes notice.)


For Household memberships, customer are redirected to a add dependent page where they are prompted to enter information regarding their household members.




If the renewal proceeds without problems, the system will automatically send a email receipt of the transaction details as provided by the users, this includes

billing information and renewal details such as length, computer start and end date of the renewal and the breakdown of cost.





If no submission occurs during the submission of the renewal form, customer then are redirected to this page which shows the summary of billing and payment regarding the renewal. It shows the Dependents summary if the customer renewed for a household membership.


Below that is the breakdown of the renewal, which are similar to the email receipt sent automatically by the system to the entered valid email address.\




In the Payment method portion, the customer then can upload a scanned copy of the Payment slip for the payment of the renewal. Which then a customer care representative from the company can verify the payment slip and activate the account.
