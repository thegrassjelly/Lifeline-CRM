Secondary Contact



The secondary contact view is essential for client users as this information will be used by the company in cases of emergency, as the company has a contact to in the side of the customer to inform in case of any unforeseen emergencies. The view includes a card view of the secondary contacts the users have added, all of which can easily by updated anytime. This requires the customer to include valid information for the company to contact.



Updating the contacts is as easy as clicking the update contact as seen in the previous page. If no problems occur, the customer would be linked to the this page

which they can make necessary changes to the information.



Adding a secondary contact has a similar view as to updating a secondary contact. Includes the same required information as seen in the capture above.
