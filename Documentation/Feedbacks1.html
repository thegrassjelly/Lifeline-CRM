Feedbacks


The feedbacks list, part of the Feedbacks sub module contains messages under the feedback messages and issue & inquiry sent by customers. As seen in the user master list and corporate accounts list, the feedbacks also has a reports printing button located at the upper right portion of the page



Clicking the details link redirects the administrator to the specifics of the message such as the complete transcript of the message sent, a drop drown of the message status which the administrator can update to Read and Unread to easily filter through the  messages.
