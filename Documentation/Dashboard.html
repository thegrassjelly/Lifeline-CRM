Account Dashboard



For none members, users will be prompted to login their account.



For registration, users have two options. One would be the manual registration. Customers who are previous users of the services of the company may choose to register as a guest or pending client.


Pending client registration require further confirmation from the company to verify their credentials. Users are required to use valid email addresses as the system will automatically send activation links for their accounts to be activated as seen the following.





The second registration method is by using the users personal Facebook account to register and skip through the email activation



It is recommended that Facebook registration further fill up their account details after registration in the profile page which is discussed in the following parts.



The Account dashboard compromises of sub parts which are designed for the ease of use of the end customers, being direct and clear to see. The main dashboard includes informative information regarding their membership information, both for individual and corporate/employer based memberships.


 


The upper right includes the Profile, Help and Log Out links



Clearing below it is the membership information bar which shows membership renewal details such as:


  • Start date
  • End date
  • Total membership length
  • Recent payment
  • Recent membership type
  • Recent membership status


After that is the corporate/employer based memberships which includes:


  • Employer name
  • Total membership length
  • Membership type



Directly below it is the article views which the customers can interact by comments.



Below each article view, customer can comment on the articles with the use of their respective Facebook accounts.



The profile page enables users to update their personal information as seen above in the capture. It includes a Employer Code input box which lets users

to enter a specific employer code to include them to a employer/corporate membership renewal plan, a different plan from Individual/Household plans.
