Medical & Dispatch Information



The Medical & Dispatch Information page are accessible for Active client users, those who are verified pending clients. Guest users cannot access this page initially. The page includes information the company has from previous client customers.


For the customers to be able to change information in the Medical & Dispatch Information page, they may need to contact the company through the Issue & Inquiry page which will be discussed next.



Medical Details page can be access if the client has previous use of the service of the company , most specifically the emergency quick response. In this page, customer can view dispatch information of all the recent dispatch, including the Initial prognosis of the responder, the date, the ambulance, dispatcher, the nurse and officers which attended to the scene of emergency.
