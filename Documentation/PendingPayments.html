Pending Payments


The pending payments gives the customer a list of previous renewal submissions which are pending for payment. Referring back the dashboard which shows a summary of recent membership, inactive and unpaid payments will not reflect unless these renewal submissions are paid and verified.


Clicking the details link will redirect the customer to the renewal detail which then they can upload their payment slip, if they haven't done yet.



Upon uploading of the payment slip for the membership renewal, it will show up which then the customer can view and verify of any corrections if there is any.



A confirmation email is also sent to the registered email address of the customer.
