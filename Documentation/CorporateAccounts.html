Corporate/Employer Accounts



Still under the user management sub module, the corporate accounts has a similar table feature to the user master list, both with the similar search and filter functionality, expanding a specific record can also show details regarding the employer/corporate account such as payment details and list of employees under the employer/corporate account.



In the rightmost part of the record is the view record button where the administrator can view specific details such as the employer information and payment history of the account. Administrators can update employer information and add payments.




The administrators can themselves upload payment/deposit slips submitted by the employers, and add payment details. This payments updates the previous record and deducts the appropriate balances from the corporate/employer accounts.




Adding corporate accounts prompts the administrator to input appropriate details as seen in the capture above.
